Cognizant Company Information

About Cognizant:
Cognizant is a global technology company founded in 1994. 
Headquartered in New Jersey, USA. It provides IT services, 
consulting, and business process services.

Leave Policy:
Employees get 18 days of paid leave per year.
Sick leave is 12 days per year.
Casual leave is 6 days per year.
Leave must be applied 2 days in advance except emergencies.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees can work from home 2 days per week.
Work from home requires manager approval.
All meetings must be attended online during WFH days.

Salary and Appraisal:
Appraisals happen every year in April.
Performance ratings are from 1 to 5.
Rating 4 and above gets 15% to 20% hike.
Rating 3 gets 8% to 10% hike.
Rating below 3 gets no hike.

Ace Team Program:
Ace Team is Cognizant's most selective engineering program.
Ace Team engineers start at 12 LPA as Associate level.
Senior Associate level starts at 18 LPA.
Ace Team works on AI native projects from day one.
Engineers build RAG pipelines and agentic workflows.
